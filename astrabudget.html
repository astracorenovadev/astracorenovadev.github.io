AstraBudget
Smart finance. Private by default.
Available on Android
Automatic expense tracking

AstraBudget reads your banking SMS and push notifications to automatically track expenses — all processing happens locally. No cloud. No data collection.

  • Auto transaction detection from SMS & push
  • ClauneckAI On-Device — proprietary AI (based on Gemma 3n, fully offline)
  • ClauneckAI Cloud — optional, powered by latest Claude 3 Haiku models for advanced insights
  • Receipt photo scanning via OCR
  • Supports currencies and languages of 25+ countries
  • Optional AI financial advice (anonymized, opt-in)
  • Encrypted local database — data never leaves your phone
